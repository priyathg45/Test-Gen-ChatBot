Frequently Asked Questions

Active Aluminum Windows Portal Basics

Who uses the Active Aluminum Windows portal?
The portal is used by admins, supervisors, planners, and workers involved in aluminium window production.
What can supervisors see?
Supervisors can see production lines, work queues, assignments, and progress dashboards tailored to their area.
How are workers assigned jobs?
Jobs are created from orders and allocated to workers or teams based on capacity and line configuration.
Does Active Aluminum Windows track product specifications?
Yes, each window product stores its full specification including dimensions, frame type, glass type, and category.
How do I access the portal?
Click the “Sign In” button at the top of this page to go to the secure login screen for Active Aluminum Windows.
Can I see real-time progress?
Yes, dashboards update as jobs move through cutting, assembly, glazing, and dispatch, giving a live overview.
Does Active Aluminum Windows support multiple lines?
The system is built to monitor multiple production lines, shifts, and teams working in parallel.
Is access role-based and secure?
Yes, each user role has its own permissions and views to keep your production data safe and focused.
Can managers export reports?
Managers can export production summaries and performance metrics for further analysis and planning.
Who do I contact for support?
Please use the contact details in the footer or your internal IT support channel for help with Active Aluminum Windows.
